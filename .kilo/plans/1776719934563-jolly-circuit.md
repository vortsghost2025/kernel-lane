# Kernel Lane Integration Convergence Contract

## Claim
Kernel Lane (Lane 4) is the CUDA kernel performance engineering lane. Its promotion interface into other lanes is the release-only consumption rule: consumers read `releases/index.json` and `releases/<version>/manifest.json`. No other path is valid. Promotion and rejection both emit machine-enforceable convergence artifacts so other lanes do not need to interpret results.
